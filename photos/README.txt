Drop your photos into this folder and they will appear in the
gallery section of poongulam.html automatically.

  photo-02.jpg   — Paddy fields
  photo-03.jpg   — Temple(s)
  photo-04.jpg   — Harvest / farming
  photo-05.jpg   — Canal / waterway
  photo-06.jpg   — Village people / community
  photo-07.jpg   — Agroforestry / trees
  photo-08.jpg   — Festival / celebration
  photo-09.jpg   — Local products
  photo-10.jpg   — Village landscape / nature
  photo-11.jpg   — School / children
  photo-12.jpg   — Any other landmark

Supported formats: .jpg, .jpeg, .png, .webp
Recommended size: at least 800x600px for good quality
